About Kosso

Built by makers,
for makers.

We started Kosso because we were tired of stitching together dozens of tools. Here's our story.

Our Mission

Simplify how teams work

We believe great software should get out of your way — giving your team the clarity and confidence to do their best work every day.

🚀

Move Faster

Reduce context switching and eliminate bottlenecks so ideas go from concept to reality in record time.

🌍

Work Anywhere

Fully remote-friendly tools with real-time sync mean your team stays aligned no matter the time zone.

💡

Stay Focused

Intelligent defaults and a clean interface keep you in flow — no bloat, no noise, just the work.

Our Values

What we stand for

These principles guide every decision we make — from product to company culture.

  • 🎯

    Clarity over cleverness

    Simple, obvious solutions beat clever ones. We optimise for the person maintaining the code tomorrow.

  • 🤲

    Default to openness

    We share our thinking early, welcome feedback, and believe transparency builds better products and teams.

  • 🌱

    Grow continuously

    Every team member is encouraged to learn, experiment, and level up — success is a shared journey.

  • ❤️

    Care deeply

    We care about our users, each other, and the long-term impact of what we build. Quality is never an afterthought.

  • 🔍

    Sweat the details

    The experience lives in the details. We pay attention to the small things because they add up to something great.

  • ⚖️

    Act with integrity

    We do what we say, own our mistakes, and hold ourselves to a high standard — even when no one's watching.
